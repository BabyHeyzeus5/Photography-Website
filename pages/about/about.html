Beginnings

My passion for photography began in high school, inspired by my mom's Canon camera. She would capture countless moments of my brothers and me during various activities growing up. As I looked through these cherished memories, I felt a growing desire to create my own.

Settling In

I soon found myself using her Canon in a similar fashion, volunteering to photograph robotics tournaments, events at my alma mater, and activities for my nonprofit. This experience deepened my appreciation for the art of photography. It was around this time that I began bringing my camera everywhere, eager to capture every moment.

Growth

Today, I am fully immersing myself in photography, exploring various styles such as street photography, portraits, and posing. Additionally, I am learning to edit photos rather than relying on filters, all in an effort to express myself creatively and have fun in the process.

Gear

Canon EOS R10

Highlights

  • Mirrorless
  • 24.2-Megapixel APS-C
  • Swing-out Screen
  • 4K Video capabilities

My digital camera of choice at the moment is the Canon EOS R10. This mirrorless offering from Canon boasts a 24.2-megapixel APS-C sensor. With its adjustable screen and intuitive controls, it allows me to quickly and easily learn the different aspects of capturing quality photos. Its Dual Pixel CMOS AF II system ensures quick and accurate autofocus, helping me take sharp, clear images effortlessly. Additionally, with 4K video recording capabilities, videography is well within reach with this camera. Overall, the Canon EOS R10 has been a significant upgrade to my photography kit.

Canon EOS Rebel Xs

Highlights

  • Film
  • Auto Focus
  • Auto Lighting

My film camera of choice at the moment is the Canon EOS Rebel XS. I picked up this camera in the summer of 2024 to delve deeper into film photography, and I’m absolutely thrilled with it. Originally introduced in the 90s, it’s a classic counterpart to the digital cameras I use. The Rebel XS features autofocus and programmable exposure modes, making it a smooth transition for me between shooting with my R10 and this film camera. Its compatibility with EF lenses also allows me to easily switch lenses between my film and digital setups.
